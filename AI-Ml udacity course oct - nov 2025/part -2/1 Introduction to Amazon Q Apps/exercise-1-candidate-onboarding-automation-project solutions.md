## Summary of Using Amazon Q Business for Document Q&A

### Overview

-   Access the application via a deployed URL.
    
-   Log in using privileged access to begin.
    
-   Upload documents to start the Q&A session.
    

### Document Upload Process

-   Click "Upload" to add files, starting with a meeting transcript.
    
-   The document, now available in Amazon Q Business, can be queried.
    

### Interactive Q&A Features

-   Utilize Amazon Q, the AI assistant, for brainstorming and text summarization.
    
-   Ask questions related to the document to understand its context and purpose.
    
-   Obtain concise information, such as one-line summaries or specific details.
    

### Case Example

-   Queries about the "Candidate Onboarding Automation Project" reveal:
    
    -   It's a kickoff meeting for automation post-offer letter processes.
        
    -   Ayesha is the technical lead.
        
    -   Provides timelines, including a 30-day delivery window.
        
    -   Identifies HR's role in UAT (User Acceptance Testing) and success criteria.
        

### Conclusion

-   Efficiently extracts necessary details without reading the entire document.
