## Sharing and Verifying Apps in Q Business

Enhance the distribution and visibility of applications within the Q Business environment by following these steps:

-   **Access Application**
    
    -   Log in to the Q Business service.
        
    -   Navigate to your published application via the Apps button.
        
-   **Share with Organization**
    
    -   Use the Share button to extend access from specific users to the entire organization.
        
    -   Customize categories to align with the company's department structure, selecting appropriate checkboxes like Marketing, Support, and Operations.
        
-   **Verify Application**
    
    -   Visit the Amazon Q apps link under the enhancements category in the Q Business console.
        
    -   Mark the application to upgrade its status to "verified."
        
-   **Confirm Verification**
    
    -   Refresh the Q Business web experience to see a blue check mark indicating the app is verified.

These processes ensure broader access and trustworthiness of applications used within organizational settings, facilitating more efficient workflow and collaboration.
