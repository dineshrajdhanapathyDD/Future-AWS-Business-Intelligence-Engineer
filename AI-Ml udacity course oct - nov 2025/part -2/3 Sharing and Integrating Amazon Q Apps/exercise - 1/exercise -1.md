Your colleague had some great ideas to improve your application and now you are going to share the app with the whole Q Business organization.

## Prerequisites

-   You will need to complete Exercise 1 in Lesson 3 before completing this Exercise.

## Exercise Scenario

-   Your colleague had some great ideas to improve your application and now you are going to share the app with the whole Q Business organization.

## High Level Steps

1.  Open Amazon Q Business and Navigating to Your Q Business App
2.  Sharing the Application with the Organization
3.  Mark the Shared App as Verified

## Detailed Instructions

### Section 1: Opening Amazon Q Business and Navigating to Your Q Business App

1.  Login to AWS console with required privileges.
2.  Navigate to Amazon Q Business service in correct AWS region.
3.  Select Applications in the navigation area.
4.  In the Applications page, select the link for the Application you created earlier.
5.  On the Application details page of your Application, click on Deployed URL link.
6.  Login using your IAM or IAM Identity Center user credentials.
7.  Select the Apps button in the left navigation.
8.  Select the name of your customer complaint application in the Apps list.

### Section 2: Sharing the Application with the Organization

1.  Select the Share button.
2.  Select the Share with All button.
3.  Select the Edit Categories button.
4.  Select the Marketing, Support, and Operations check boxes, and select Save.
5.  Close the  _Share your app_  dialog box.
6.  Select the Library button in the left navigation.
7.  Confirm that the App appears in the selected categories, and the All Apps category, but no others.

### Section 3: Marking the Shared App as Verified

1.  In the Q Business console navigation area, under Enhancements, select Amazon Q Apps.
2.  Select the checkbox for the published App.
3.  Select Update state, and then select Verified.
4.  Refresh the Q Business Web Experience, and confirm that the app carries the blue checkmark that identifies a Verified app.
